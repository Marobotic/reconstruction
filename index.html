Aion RP is an artistic endeavour by fans of the original game to recreate what Aion seemed to develop into in its early years. We’ve tasked ourselves with reworking early content and broken systems according to lore and modern standards of gameplay in order to deliver a serene immersive experience in a coherent believable world. We are making a PvE Role-Play server where you can come to relax and escape.

Many of this game’s problems stem from it originally being a subscription-based mmo. The more time players spent in the game, the more they had to pay. Malicious time wasters fill every lazy nook and cranny of it. We identify these and other unfun things in the game and fundamentally rework them from the ground up.

  • • Increased basic movement and flight speed to that of endgame values. You no longer need boots to run fast. Buffs, debuffs and gear stats have been scaled accordingly. Monster speeds have been bumped up to match player speed aswell, causing the game to start feeling much less janky overall. This change also makes it viable to make tall and human-proportioned characters, as now their running animation works at quite a satisfying speed. •Removed title stats in order to make titles purely cosmetic.
